Kong: Skull Island

The film takes the viewer to the 70s of the last century. The protagonist organizes an expedition to the mysterious Skull Island, which is inhabited by giant creatures. The main character is a photographer who wants to capture the beauty of the island and its inhabitants. However, the expedition turns into a nightmare when they encounter the giant ape Kong.
